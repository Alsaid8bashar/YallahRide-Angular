Our Story

Once upon a time in Irbid, two brilliant students named Hasan Al Shannag and Bashar Al Said embarked on their final project at the Jordan University of Science and Technology. They were determined to address the transportation challenges in their city, and thus, the YallahRide website was born.

With their combined knowledge and passion for technology, Hasan and Bashar designed YallahRide as a platform connecting drivers and passengers for carpooling. Their vision was to optimize travel efficiency, reduce traffic congestion, and foster a sense of community among commuters.

Through countless hours of coding and testing, Hasan and Bashar created an intuitive website. Users could register as either drivers or passengers, input their travel preferences, and easily find matches. The duo incorporated advanced features such as secure payments and user ratings, ensuring a safe and reliable experience for all participants.

Upon presenting YallahRide to their professors and peers, Hasan and Bashar received resounding applause and praise. Their innovative solution and dedication to tackling transportation issues left an indelible mark on their university community.

With their graduation project, Hasan and Bashar felt immense pride. Their journey at the university had shaped them into problem solvers, innovators, and community builders. Their story served as an inspiration to future students, encouraging them to dream big, tackle real-world challenges, and create meaningful solutions.
